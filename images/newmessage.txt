    <h1>Our Message: Clean Water for Every Filipino</h1>
    <p>Access to safe water is not a luxury – it is a basic human right. Yet millions in the Philippines still suffer from water insecurity, pollution, and lack of sanitation. We advocate for immediate, sustainable, and community‑led solutions.</p>

    <h2>7 Major Water Problems in the Philippines</h2>
    <ul class="problem-list">
        <li><strong>1. Contaminated groundwater</strong> – Heavy metals and bacteria from improper waste disposal.</li>
        <li><strong>2. Unsafe drinking water in rural areas</strong> – 1 in 10 Filipinos rely on unsafe sources.</li>
        <li><strong>3. Water pollution from factories and mines</strong> – Rivers like Pasig and Marilao are heavily polluted.</li>
        <li><strong>4. Inadequate sewage and sanitation</strong> – Only ~10% of households are connected to sewerage systems.</li>
        <li><strong>5. Seasonal water scarcity</strong> – El Niño causes severe droughts in parts of Luzon and Mindanao.</li>
        <li><strong>6. Plastic waste choking waterways</strong> – Philippines is one of the top ocean plastic polluters.</li>
        <li><strong>7. Poor water governance</strong> – Leaking pipes, corruption, and lack of data transparency.</li>
    </ul>
