    <h1>📌 About This Site</h1>
    <p><strong>What it is:</strong> An educational advocacy website promoting clean water access and sanitation in the Philippines.</p>
    <p><strong>For what:</strong> To inform visitors about water insecurity, pollution, and the urgent need for action. We aim to drive awareness and encourage support for water-related projects.</p>
    <p><strong>Why it was made:</strong> Millions of Filipinos lack access to safely managed drinking water. This site compiles key problems, reliable data, and a clear call to action – because change starts with knowledge.</p>
    <h2>Core Advocacy Points (limited to 7)</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>* Universal access to clean drinking water</li>
        <li>* Ending waterborne diseases</li>
        <li>* Protecting rivers, lakes, and groundwater from pollution</li>
        <li>* Climate‑resilient water infrastructure</li>
        <li>* Proper sanitation for every community</li>
        <li>* Hygiene education in schools and barangays</li>
        <li>* Government accountability and water transparency</li>
    </ul>
