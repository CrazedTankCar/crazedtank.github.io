Reliable Sources

The information on this site is based on the following credible reports and organizations:

  • World Health Organization (WHO) – Drinking-water facts and WASH data
  • UNICEF Philippines – Water, sanitation and hygiene in the Philippines
  • Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) – Annual poverty and water access surveys
  • Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) – Water quality reports
  • Asian Development Bank (ADB) – Water security in Southeast Asia
  • Water.org – Philippine water crisis overview
  • National Water Resources Board (NWRB) – Groundwater and supply data
