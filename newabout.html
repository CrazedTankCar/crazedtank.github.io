About This Site

What it is: An educational advocacy website promoting clean water access and sanitation in the Philippines.

For what: To inform visitors about water insecurity, pollution, and the urgent need for action. We aim to drive awareness and encourage support for water-related projects.

Why it was made: Millions of Filipinos lack access to safely managed drinking water. This site compiles key problems, reliable data, and a clear call to action because change starts with knowledge.

Important Advocacy Takeaways:

  • Universal access to clean drinking water
  • Ending waterborne diseases
  • Protecting rivers, lakes, and groundwater from pollution
  • Climate‑resilient water infrastructure
  • Proper sanitation for every community
  • Hygiene education in schools and barangays
  • Government accountability and water transparency
